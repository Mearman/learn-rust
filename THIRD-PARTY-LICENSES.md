## Rustlings

Some Fix exercises (`src/data/fix-exercises.ts`) are adapted from
[Rustlings](https://github.com/rust-lang/rustlings). Each adapted exercise is
marked in the source with an `// adapted: rustlings/<name>` comment. The broken
snippets are derived from Rustlings exercises; the hint progression and
idiomatic notes are original to this project.
